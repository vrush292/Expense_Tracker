How to Use the Expense Tracker Application

Getting Started

To start using the application:

  1. Create an account or log in if you already have one.
  2. Navigate to the Income section to add your income sources.
  3. Go to the Expense section to log your expenses.
  4. Set your budget in the Budget section to manage your spending.
  5. Define your financial goals in the Goals section and monitor your progress.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I add my income?

To add your income, go to the Income section and click on "Add Income." Fill in the required details and save.

2. Can I set a budget for different categories?

Yes! In the Budget section, you can set budgets for various categories such as groceries, entertainment, etc.

3. How can I track my expenses effectively?

To track your expenses, navigate to the Expense section and click on "Add Expense." Enter the details of your expense and save it to keep a record.

4. What if I exceed my budget?

If you exceed your budget, the application will notify you. You can then adjust your spending or modify your budget as needed.

5. How do I set financial goals?

To set financial goals, go to the Goals section and click on "Add Goal." Fill in the details and track your progress over time.
